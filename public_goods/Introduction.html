Welcome to the Public Goods Game

In this experiment, you will be part of a group of {{ C.PLAYERS_PER_GROUP }} players.

Your Endowment

You will receive an initial endowment of {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} points.

How the Game Works

You must decide how much of your endowment to contribute to a group project. You can contribute any amount between 0 and {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} points.

Group Project Returns

All contributions made by group members will be added together and multiplied by an efficiency factor of {{ C.MULTIPLIER }}. The resulting amount will then be divided equally among all {{ C.PLAYERS_PER_GROUP }} group members, regardless of how much each person contributed.

Calculating Your Payoff

Your final payoff will be calculated as:

Your Payoff = Your endowment - Your contribution + Your share from the group project

Example

If everyone in the group contributes 50 points:

  • Total contributions = 3 × 50 = 150 points
  • Multiplied amount = 150 × 1.8 = 270 points
  • Each person's share = 270 ÷ 3 = 90 points
  • Your payoff = 100 - 50 + 90 = 140 points

Note: Your decisions are anonymous. Other group members will not know your identity or your specific contribution.
